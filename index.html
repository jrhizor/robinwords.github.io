Thanks for a great 11 years!

While the original purpose of the site was to allow people to write stories collaboratively, I added a small word minigame that took off in popularity, leading to almost four person-years of time spent on the website in the past eleven years.

Since there hasn't been much activity on the rest of the site and the core infrastructure is very old, I decided to shut down the site's interactive functionality.

The game is still available to be played, and I'll maintain this static archive of the stories for the forseeable future.

Thank you everyone for your contributions (from suggestions for the game dictionary to the stories themselves).

— Jared, Nov 2019
